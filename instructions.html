How To Use This API

There are 4 super tasty dishes in this API:

  1. Spaghetti
  2. Fish Sticks
  3. Meatloaf
  4. Chicken and Rice
Please go to http://localhost:3000/dishes/Spaghetti for the ingredients and the cook time of our yummy Spaghetti.
Please go to http://localhost:3000/dishes/Fish%20Sticks for the ingredients and the cook time of our yummy SpagFishstickshetti.
Please go to http://localhost:3000/dishes/Meatloaf for the ingredients and the cook time of our yummy Meatloaf.
Please go to http://localhost:3000/dishes/Chicken%20And%20Rice for the ingredients and the cook time of our yummy Chicken and Rice.
